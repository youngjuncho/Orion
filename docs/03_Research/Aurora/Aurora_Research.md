# Aurora Research Specification

Version: 0.1

Status: Research

Author: Orion OS

Last Updated: 2026-06-21

---

# Purpose

Aurora is the market climate framework of Orion OS.

Aurora exists to measure the current investment environment and detect changes in market conditions.

Aurora does not generate investment recommendations.

Aurora provides environmental context for investment decisions.

---

# Core Question

Aurora seeks to answer:

"What environment are we investing in?"

---

# Design Philosophy

## Observe More, Predict Less

Aurora evaluates observable market conditions.

Aurora does not forecast future prices.

---

## State Over News

Market state is more important than daily news flow.

Aurora focuses on measurable conditions rather than narratives.

---

## Regime Awareness

Market conditions change over time.

Aurora seeks to identify both:

* Current Regime
* Regime Transition

---

# Research Objectives

Aurora should identify:

* Risk On Environments
* Neutral Environments
* Risk Off Environments

Aurora should also monitor:

* Improving Conditions
* Stable Conditions
* Deteriorating Conditions

---
