# Purpose

This document maintains the inventory of indicators considered for Aurora.

Indicators are grouped into:

* Candidate
* Approved
* Retired

The objective is transparency and governance.

---

# Indicator Lifecycle

## Candidate

Being researched.

Not used in production.

---

## Approved

Official Aurora indicator.

May be used in scoring.

---

## Retired

Previously used or evaluated.

No longer active.
