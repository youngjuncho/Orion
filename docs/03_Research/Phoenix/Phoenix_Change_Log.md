# Purpose

This document records historical leadership changes, replacement risk events, category evolution, and portfolio decisions within Phoenix.

The objective is to maintain an auditable record of leadership transitions.

---

# Event Types

## LEADER_CHANGE

Category leader replaced.

---

## CHALLENGER_CHANGE

Primary challenger replaced.

---

## RISK_ESCALATION

Replacement risk increased significantly.

---

## RISK_REDUCTION

Replacement risk decreased significantly.

---

## CATEGORY_ADDED

New category approved.

---

## CATEGORY_REMOVED

Category removed.

---

## PORTFOLIO_CHANGE

Portfolio rebalanced.
