# Purpose

This document defines the operational review process used by Phoenix.

---

# Review Levels

Monthly Review

Quarterly Review

Annual Review

---

# Monthly Review

Objective:

Monitor leadership changes.

Review Items:

* Leader score
* Challenger score
* Replacement risk
* Trend direction

Possible Outcomes:

* No Action
* Monitor
* Review Required

---

# Quarterly Review

Objective:

Evaluate category health.

Review Items:

* Candidate universe
* Category strength
* Emerging challengers
* Retired candidates

Possible Outcomes:

* Universe update
* Category update
* No Action

---

# Annual Review

Objective:

Evaluate framework effectiveness.

Review Items:

* Category relevance
* Scoring methodology
* Portfolio construction methodology
* Governance process

Possible Outcomes:

* Framework update
* No Action

---

# Review Triggers

Leader replacement

Replacement risk escalation

Category deterioration

New candidate emergence

---

# Review Outputs

Leader Status

Challenger Status

Replacement Risk

Recommended Actions
