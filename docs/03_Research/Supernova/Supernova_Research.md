# Purpose

This document defines the research foundation of Supernova.

Supernova is the long-term equity accumulation framework of Orion OS.

The framework seeks to identify and accumulate ownership in companies that are positioned to benefit from major structural transformations in the global economy.

---

# Core Question

Supernova exists to answer a single question:

Which companies are most likely to dominate the future?

---

# Investment Philosophy

Supernova is based on the belief that long-term wealth is created by owning exceptional businesses during periods of structural economic change.

The framework focuses on:

* Long-term ownership
* Business quality
* Competitive advantage
* Structural growth
* Capital compounding

Supernova does not attempt to predict short-term market movements.

Supernova does not attempt to trade market cycles.

---

# Orion 5D Framework

Supernova is built upon the Orion 5D Megatrend Framework.

The five structural themes are:

## Decoupling

The fragmentation of global economic and technological systems into competing geopolitical blocs.

Examples:

* National security
* Defense technology
* Strategic manufacturing
* Semiconductor independence

---

## Deglobalization

The reshoring and regionalization of supply chains.

Examples:

* Industrial automation
* Logistics infrastructure
* Domestic manufacturing
* Supply chain resilience

---

## Demographics

Long-term population and labor force changes.

Examples:

* Healthcare
* Biotechnology
* Longevity
* Automation

---

## Decarbonization

The transition toward lower-carbon energy systems.

Examples:

* Nuclear power
* Renewable energy
* Grid infrastructure
* Energy efficiency

---

## Digital Transformation

The digitization of economic activity.

Examples:

* Artificial Intelligence
* Cloud Computing
* Cybersecurity
* Semiconductors
* Data Infrastructure

---

# Research Focus

Supernova seeks companies that benefit directly from one or more 5D themes.

The framework prioritizes:

* Structural growth
* Category leadership
* Durable moats
* Strategic relevance
* Long-term earnings expansion

# Exclusions

Supernova does not focus on:

* Short-term trading
* Technical analysis
* Market timing
* Macroeconomic forecasting
* Cryptocurrency projects
* ETF allocation

These functions belong to other Orion frameworks.
