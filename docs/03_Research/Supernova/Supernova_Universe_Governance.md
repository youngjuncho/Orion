# Purpose

This document defines governance rules for the Supernova Candidate Universe.

The objective is to ensure consistent evaluation of long-term theme leaders.

---

# Governance Scope

This document governs:

* Candidate additions
* Candidate removals
* Theme changes
* Universe reviews

---

# Governance Principles

Supernova shall maintain:

* Theme relevance
* Strategic significance
* Financial quality
* Long-term durability

---

# Candidate Lifecycle

Candidate

↓

Approved Company

↓

Leader

↓

Retired

---

# Candidate Addition Criteria

A company may be added if:

* Theme relevance is significant
* Market position is meaningful
* Financial quality is acceptable
* Long-term viability is demonstrated

---

# Candidate Removal Criteria

A company may be removed if:

* Theme relevance deteriorates
* Competitive position weakens materially
* Financial quality declines significantly

---

# Theme Addition Criteria

A theme may be added if:

* Long-term structural significance exists
* Sufficient candidate companies exist
* Theme persistence is expected

---

# Theme Removal Criteria

A theme may be removed if:

* Long-term relevance disappears
* Candidate universe becomes insufficient

---

# Review Authority

Universe changes require:

* Research review
* Documentation update
* Decision Log entry

before implementation.

---

# Review Frequency

Universe Review:

Quarterly

Theme Review:

Quarterly

Framework Review:

Annually

---

# Decision Logging

All material changes must be documented in:

Decision_Log.md

before implementation.
