# Purpose

This document defines the theme structure used by Supernova.

Supernova evaluates companies through the Orion 5D Megatrend Framework.

Principle:

Megatrend First

Company Second

---

# Theme Evaluation Structure

Supernova evaluates:

Megatrend

↓

Theme

↓

Category

↓

Company

↓

Watchlist

---

# Theme 1

Decoupling

---

## Description

The separation of economic, technological, and geopolitical systems into competing blocs.

---

## Investment Logic

Nations increasingly prioritize:

* National security
* Strategic autonomy
* Technology sovereignty
* Domestic industrial capability

---

## Key Categories

* Defense Technology
* Aerospace
* Strategic Manufacturing
* Semiconductor Sovereignty

---

## Representative Companies

* Palantir
* RTX
* Lockheed Martin
* TSMC

---

## Status

Active

---

# Theme 2

Deglobalization

---

## Description

The restructuring of global supply chains toward regional resilience.

---

## Investment Logic

Companies benefit from:

* Reshoring
* Nearshoring
* Supply chain redundancy
* Domestic production

---

## Key Categories

* Industrial Automation
* Logistics
* Manufacturing Equipment
* Infrastructure

---

## Representative Companies

* Rockwell Automation
* Schneider Electric
* Siemens

---

## Status

Active

---

* Automation

---

## Key Categories

* Robotics

Active

# Theme States

Each theme receives one of the following states.

Emerging

Developing

Established

Mature

Declining

---

# Company Eligibility

A company should have meaningful exposure to at least one approved theme.

Companies with exposure to multiple themes may receive higher strategic priority.

Example:

Microsoft

Themes:

* Digital Transformation
* AI

---

GE Vernova

Themes:

* Decarbonization
* Infrastructure

---

# Theme Review Frequency

Theme Review

Quarterly

---

Framework Review

Annually

# Governance Rules

New themes may be added only when:

* Structural relevance is demonstrated
* Long-term investment logic exists
* Sufficient public data is available

Changes must be recorded in:

Decision_Log.md

before implementation.
