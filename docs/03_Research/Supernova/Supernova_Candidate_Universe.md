# Purpose

This document defines the candidate universe used by Supernova.

Supernova does not evaluate all public companies.

Instead, Supernova evaluates a curated universe of companies associated with approved 5D themes.

Leadership evaluation occurs only within the candidate universe.

# Candidate Inclusion Criteria

Candidate companies should satisfy most of the following:

* Direct exposure to a 5D theme
* Strategic importance
* Strong market position
* Sufficient liquidity
* Reliable financial data

# Candidate Addition Rules

New companies may be added if:

* Theme relevance increases
* Strategic importance grows
* Long-term leadership potential emerges

All additions must be documented in:

Decision_Log.md

before implementation.

---

# Candidate Removal Rules

Candidates may be removed if:

* Theme relevance deteriorates
* Competitive position weakens materially
* Financial quality becomes unacceptable

---

# Relationship With Approved Companies

Candidate Universe represents the research universe.

Approved Companies represent active ownership.

All Approved Companies must originate from the Candidate Universe.

---

# Relationship With Watchlist

Watchlist monitoring is performed within the Candidate Universe.

Leader and Challenger selection are derived from the Candidate Universe.
