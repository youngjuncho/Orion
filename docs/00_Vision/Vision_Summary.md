# Orion OS Vision Summary

## Vision

Orion OS is a Personal Investment Operating System designed to help investors understand market conditions and portfolio status within one minute.

The objective is not to predict the future.

The objective is to observe the current state of markets, identify actionable signals, and support systematic investment decisions.

---

## Core Philosophy

### Observe More, Predict Less

Investment decisions should be based on observable market states rather than forecasts.

### Systems Over Opinions

Repeatable systems are preferred over subjective judgment.

### Signals Over Information

The goal is not to consume more information but to identify meaningful signals.

### State Over News

Market state matters more than daily news flow.

---

## Mission

Provide a daily investment operating system that answers four questions:

### Aurora

What is the current market climate?

### Moon

What assets should be held right now?

### Supernova

Which dominant companies are positioned to benefit from long-term structural megatrends?

### Phoenix

Which digital assets are emerging as category leaders within the evolving crypto ecosystem?

---

# Orion Frameworks

## Aurora

Monitoring Framework

Purpose:

* Monitor macroeconomic conditions
* Detect market regimes
* Measure liquidity and risk appetite
* Provide context for portfolio engines

Outputs:

* Aurora Score
* Market Regime
* Risk State
* Transition Risk

Aurora does not manage portfolios.

---

## Moon

ETF Portfolio Engine

Purpose:

* Tactical portfolio management
* Monthly rebalancing
* Evidence-based asset allocation

Planned Strategies:

* ADM
* BAA
* BDA
* HAA
* VAA

---

## Supernova

Equity Portfolio Engine

Purpose:

* Identify dominant companies positioned to benefit from long-term structural trends
* Support long-term accumulation investing
* Monitor moat deterioration and leadership changes

5D Framework:

* Decoupling
* Deglobalization
* Demographics
* Decarbonization
* Digital Transformation

Investment Policy:

* Monthly accumulation
* Long-term holding
* Replace holdings only when the approved company list changes

---

## Phoenix

Digital Asset Portfolio Engine

Purpose:

* Monitor category-leading digital assets
* Identify emerging crypto leaders
* Track ecosystem strength and adoption
* Own approved altcoin category leaders

Current Focus Areas:

* Smart Contract Platforms
* Oracle Networks
* Real World Assets
* AI Infrastructure
* Data Availability

Portfolio Policy:

* BTC and ETH are Core Digital Assets outside Phoenix governance.
* Phoenix manages approved altcoin category leaders.
* Challengers and watchlist assets are monitored but not held.
* Approved Phoenix category leaders are equally weighted.

References:

* D-021
* D-022

---

# End State

Each morning, Orion OS should enable the investor to understand:

* Asset Allocation Status
* Market Climate Status
* Megatrend Status
* Digital Asset Status

within one minute.

The final objective is to build a complete Personal Investment Operating System.
