# Phoenix Operating Model

## Purpose

This document describes the current operating model of Phoenix within Orion OS.

Phoenix is Orion's digital asset framework.

The purpose of Phoenix is to identify, monitor, and accumulate category-leading digital assets outside of BTC, ETH, and SOL.

---

# Objective

Phoenix exists to answer a single question:

"Which digital assets are becoming category leaders?"

The objective is long-term exposure to emerging digital infrastructure and ecosystem leaders.

---

# Investment Universe

Phoenix focuses on digital assets outside of:

* BTC
* ETH
* SOL

These assets are managed separately and are not considered part of the Phoenix universe.

---

# Selection Philosophy

Phoenix does not attempt to predict short-term price movements.

The objective is to identify category leaders within important digital asset sectors.

Examples:

* AI Infrastructure
* Decentralized Finance
* Real World Assets
* Oracle Networks
* Compute Networks
* Identity Infrastructure
